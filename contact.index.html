Our Mission

Our mission, here at New-Crypto, is to provide beginners a guide to the functionality, useful, and history of cryptocurrency. Crypto is a major topic that is increasingly becoming popular and gaining interest by the majority of people. In a world where many of the major forms of spending and communicating is virtual, cryptocurrency provides a way for that style to be implemented. The topic of finance is one that is ever increasing and demands people from over the world to first learn of it in a simplified way. New-Crypto demonstrates this since it simplifies the information related to cryptocurrency in a way where the average computer user can visit and gain a base of interest in cryptocurrency without being overloaded with a wide and complex set of info. In New-Crypto, we provide a basis of what cyrptocurrency is, the different trends associated with crypto, the history behind its popularity, and pages dedicated to Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Tether each. These are valuable components that help users gain a sense of the different aspects of cryptos.
